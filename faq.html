Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about attending TEDxDPS Monarch Intl School Youth.

Registration & Tickets

Do I need a ticket to attend? +

Yes, registration is mandatory. While the event is free, we have limited capacity (100 seats) and require advance registration to ensure proper planning. Register through our website to secure your spot.

Is the event really free? +

Yes! TEDxDPS Monarch is completely free to attend. There are no hidden costs or fees. We believe ideas worth spreading should be accessible to everyone.

How do I register? +

Simply fill out the registration form on our Register page. You'll receive a confirmation email once your registration is processed.

Can I register on the day of the event? +

Walk-in registrations may be accommodated only if seats are available. However, we strongly recommend pre-registering to guarantee your spot as we expect to reach full capacity.

What if I registered but can't attend? +

Please email us at tedxdpsmonarch@gmail.com as soon as possible so we can open your spot to someone on the waitlist. We appreciate your consideration!

Event Details

When and where is the event? +

December 13, 2025, from 3:00 PM to 7:00 PM at DPS Monarch International School, Doha, Qatar. Doors open at 2:30 PM for registration.

What time should I arrive? +

We recommend arriving by 2:45 PM to complete check-in and settle in before the opening ceremony at 3:00 PM. This also gives you time to network with other attendees.

How long is the event? +

The main program runs from 3:00 PM to 7:00 PM (4 hours), including 9 speaker sessions, a networking break, and closing ceremony. Check our Schedule page for detailed timing.

What's the dress code? +

Smart casual. Wear something comfortable yet presentable. Think business casual or neat casual attire.

Will food be provided? +

Light refreshments and snacks will be provided during the networking break. Water will be available throughout the event.

Speakers & Content

Who are the speakers? +

We have 9 incredible speakers including entrepreneurs, innovators, students, and thought leaders. Visit our Speakers page to learn more about each speaker and their topics.

Can I apply to be a speaker? +

Our speaker lineup for this event has been finalized. However, stay connected with us for future opportunities! Follow us on Instagram @tedxdpsmonarchintlschool for updates.

Will talks be recorded? +

Yes! All talks will be professionally recorded and may be published on the TEDx YouTube channel after the event. Photography will also take place throughout the day.

What topics will be covered? +

Our theme is "Defy to Define." Topics range from AI and technology to psychology, leadership, sustainability, and personal growth. Each talk challenges conventional thinking and inspires action.

Logistics & Accessibility

Where can I park? +

Parking is available at DPS Monarch International School. Detailed parking instructions will be sent in your confirmation email.

Is the venue accessible? +

Yes, the venue is wheelchair accessible. If you have specific accessibility needs, please contact us in advance at tedxdpsmonarch@gmail.com so we can ensure your comfort.

Can I bring children? +

The event content is designed for teens and adults. Children are welcome but must be supervised at all times and should be able to sit through 4 hours of talks.

What should I bring? +

Just yourself and an open mind! We recommend bringing a notebook if you like to take notes. Your registration confirmation (digital or printed) will help speed up check-in.

Can I take photos? +

Yes! We encourage you to share your experience on social media using #TEDxDPSMonarch. However, please silence your devices during talks and avoid flash photography.

About TEDx

What is TEDx? +

TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. The "x" means independently organized. Learn more on our About page.

How is TEDx different from TED? +

TED is the global organization. TEDx events are independently organized under a license from TED, following TED's format and guidelines while featuring local speakers and organizers.

Who organizes this event? +

TEDxDPS Monarch Intl School Youth is organized by a passionate team of student and faculty organizers from DPS Monarch International School. Meet our team here.
