About TEDxDPS Monarch Youth

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience.

Our Mission

TEDxDPS Monarch Intl School Youth is a platform for young minds to share bold, innovative ideas that challenge the status quo and inspire meaningful change. We believe students have powerful voices that deserve to be heard.

What is TEDx?

At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized.

Our Theme

DEFY to Define

Push boundaries. Challenge norms. Redefine what's possible. Our theme celebrates the courage to break free from conventional thinking and forge new pathways.
