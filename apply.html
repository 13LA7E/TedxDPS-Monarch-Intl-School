Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a ticket to attend? +

No physical ticket is required. Once you register, you'll receive a confirmation email. Simply present this confirmation (digital or printed) at the venue for entry.

Is there an age limit? +

While this is a youth-focused event, we welcome attendees of all ages. Students, parents, educators, and community members are all encouraged to attend.

Can I register multiple people? +

Yes! You can register up to 5 attendees in a single registration. Each person will be covered under the same confirmation.

What should I bring on the day? +

Bring your registration confirmation, an open mind, and your curiosity! We recommend arriving 15-30 minutes early for check-in.

Will the talks be recorded? +

Yes! All talks will be professionally recorded and shared after the event, allowing the ideas to reach an even wider audience.

Can I apply to be a speaker? +

Our speaker lineup for December 13, 2025 has been finalized. However, stay connected with us for future opportunities to share your ideas on the TEDx stage.

What if I need to cancel my registration? +

If you can't make it, please email us at tedxdpsmonarch@gmail.com so we can open your seat for someone on the waitlist. We appreciate your consideration!
