Our Speakers

Meet the visionaries, innovators, and change-makers who will share ideas worth spreading at TEDxDPS Monarch Intl School Youth.

HA

Mr. Haitham Al-Haidari

Entrepreneur & Former CEO
Qatar Living

"Defying Conventional Career Paths"

Visionary entrepreneur who transformed Qatar Living into the region's leading community platform, sharing insights on building businesses that connect communities and challenge traditional career trajectories.

CA

Mr. Chandrashekar Angadi

Engineer & Stand-up Comedian
ABB

"Breaking the Engineer Stereotype"

A unique blend of technical expertise and comedic talent, Chandrashekar proves that engineers can be creative storytellers, combining his work at ABB with a thriving stand-up comedy career that defies stereotypes.

NS

Mr. Neeshad Shafi

Environmentalist
Arab Youth Climate Movement Qatar

"Empowering Youth Voices in Climate Action"

Leading climate activist mobilizing Arab youth to take action on environmental challenges, demonstrating how young voices can drive meaningful change in addressing the climate crisis.

STUDENT SPEAKER
AG

Ms. Avika Gaur

Head Girl & Secretary-General
Monarch MUN 2025, DPS Monarch Intl School

"Redefining Student Leadership"

As Head Girl and Secretary-General of Monarch MUN 2025, Avika embodies the next generation of leaders, sharing her journey of balancing academic excellence with meaningful student advocacy and diplomacy.
